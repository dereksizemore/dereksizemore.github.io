My Story

My career in web design started when I wrote my first HTML web page in notepad in 2002. I got my first PC for Christmas a year earlier and it changed my life forever. I dove into a world of learning that scratched an itch I'd have for my entire life.


My first major milestone was designing, coding, and maintaining my schools website with another classmate during my last 2 years of High School in 2007 and 2008. Before that I had just finished editing my first wordpress template through Upwork (formerly E-lance) as a freelancer in 2006. After High School I made a few web pages for college classmates projects in 2009 and 2010. My next major milestone was selling a web page to Circle P Whitetails while trying to start my business Derek's Web Pages in 2010. Finally, I worked on several freelance projects that consisted of building webpages with wordpress and other site-builders, updating content for static web pages, and designing graphics and logo's between 2011 and 2014.


My career decisions and life choices have taken me away from web design at times, yet I always seem to find my way back. In April of 2022, I finished designing, coding, and implementing the first iteration of a brand and website, Pua Preserve. This project has once again re-ignited my passion for development and started me on the path towards mastering Javascript and React. I pride myself on being an agile learner, passionate teacher, and persistent problem solver.
